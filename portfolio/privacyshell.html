Privacy Shell Inc.

  • April 2015
  • Web Dev | UX Design | Branding

Privacy Shell is a site where users learn the latest news about online privacy. It is also a service where users can connect with our privacy experts and browse through our suggested solutions and install the products that best suit their needs to have better control of their online privacy.

My responsibilities were to implement the sites and landing pages, to design the concept, branding, logo, UI/UX flow and to implement analytics tracking, user testing and A/B testing optimizations.
